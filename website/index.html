Skin Sense

Skin Sense is an app that uses machine learning to detect the three most common types of skin cancer: Basal Cell Caricinoma, Squamous Cell Caricinoma, and Malignant Melanoma from a photograph. The neural network takes into account the olor, patterns, and clusters of a persons's skin. Upload an image to recieve a classification by the neural network.
